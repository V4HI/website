Frequently Asked Questions

What is health insurance?

Health insurance is a type of insurance coverage that pays for medical and surgical expenses incurred by the insured.

Why do I need health insurance?

Health insurance can protect you from high, unexpected medical costs.

How do I get health insurance?

You can get health insurance through your employer, through a government program, or by purchasing it on your own.
